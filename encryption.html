Keeping Data Private

Encryption is a method of protecting information by turning it into unreadable text that can only be unlocked with a special key. This helps keep data safe when it is stored or sent over the internet.

Many everyday services, such as online banking and messaging apps, rely on encryption to keep user information private. Without encryption, sensitive data could be easily stolen.
