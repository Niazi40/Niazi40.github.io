What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from digital attacks. Today, almost everything depends on technology, from banking to communication, so keeping systems secure is very important.

Security threats can come from many places, including hackers, malicious software, or even simple mistakes made by users. Cybersecurity focuses on preventing these problems and reducing the damage when something goes wrong. This field combines technology, rules, and good habits to keep information safe.
